About Us.


What Is Porsche ?

Porsche is a German car manufacturer renowned worldwide for its specialty in producing luxury, high-performance sports cars. In short, this is the definition and essence of Porsche. Dr. Ing. h.c. F. Porsche AG. is the founder of Porsche. It is based in Germany, with its headquarters in Stuttgart. Their main focus is producing sports cars (such as the iconic 911 and 718 Cayman/Boxster), but their product line also includes luxury SUVs (such as the Cayenne and Macan), sedans/Gran Turismos (the Panamera), and high-speed all-electric cars (the Taycan).


Porsche's philosophy is synonymous with combining racing performance (they hold numerous racing records, including at Le Mans) with everyday usability. Porsche cars are known for their timeless design (especially the 911), precise engineering, and superior handling. Essentially, when you hear the word "Porsche," what comes to mind is the iconic German luxury sports car brand focused on an exceptional driving experience.
